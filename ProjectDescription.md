#Final Project Description

## Theme
Fishing based game. The user will focus on fishing instead of farming or mining, where they want to gain both money from catching fish, and fame from catching legendary fish.

## Limited Resources/Time
Time wil be the main one, with the user having one shortened year to catch as much fish as they can. Another will be a hunger system where they need to catch at least a certain amount of fish each day to keep themselves fed.

## Iventory
The user will have an inventory with that will contain their items. They will have a fishing rod, and many fish inside. The fish can be used to sell, eat, and give for quests.

## Community Center Bundle
This will of course, be made up of fish. The player will need to have caught every legendary fish to fullfill this bundle by the end of the year.

##Characters
The characters planned so far will be: One to upgrade your fishing rod with money, One to give certain fish to for extra money, One to tell the player information on what to do, and one to sell fish too.

## Locations
The locations will be: The mountain (for lake fish), The ocean (for sea fish), The river (guess what fish will be here), The pond (for pond fish), and The sewers (for special fish). Also the community center to check bundel progress.

## JoJaMart
You can find the joja mart person in the sewers, and if you buy their special fishing rod, it will make fishing much easier. The trade off will be a bad ending that has every thing you caught with the special fishing rod repossesed by joja mart, leaving you broke and homeless.

## Loop
Every day, you can spend time fishing to catch fish and make money, which you can use to eat and buy upgrades for your fishing rod.

## Ending
After a year has passed you will get one of a few differnt endings. First is the good ending if you made it past a certain threshhold of money and also compelted the community center. Second and third are the okay endings where you only met one of the two goals. Fourth is the bad endign where you didn't meet either of your goals. 5th is the bad joja mart ending where you made deals with them.

## Classes
Player, Items, Character, Game, Quest, Inventory, Bundle, Location, Fishing_Rod.
